To-Do List App

The To-Do List App provides a simple and intuitive way to manage tasks on the go. It allows users to organize their tasks by categories like Today, This Week, Work, and Home. The app also features task prioritization with color-coded labels, making it easy to see important tasks at a glance. Users can add, edit, and manage tasks with dates, times, and categories, ensuring they stay on top of their responsibilities. With a clean interface and smooth navigation, this app enhances productivity while keeping task management straightforward and user-friendly.

E-library

The E-library app offers a convenient way to access all your books digitally, allowing users to explore a wide variety of titles at their fingertips. The app features a clean, user-friendly interface, displayed on a tablet with easy navigation options. Users can browse through different categories of books, and the image shows a hand selecting a specific category. The E-library enhances reading experiences by providing access to a vast library anytime, anywhere, making it a go-to platform for book lovers and students alike.
